Slots Machine - Read Me File

About:
This program is a digital slot machine, you start with $2000 and you can choose to gamble it. Three random numbers will
be generated, and you can win chips based on the numbers presented. You can also view the credits and your own statistics,
which include: How many times you have played, how much you have won, how much you have lost, how many times you have lost
all your chips and the amount of chips you have bet.

Winnings:
If you get 2 of the same number you win 3 times your bet,
If you get 3 of the same number you win 5 times your bet,
If all 3 of the numbers are 7's then you win the jackot (10 times your bet),
and if you get all different numbers you lose your bet.

How to play:
From the main menu you select an option either 1,2,3 or 4 by pressing the corresponding number then enter. To choose a yes
or no option you can type: yes, Yes, Y or y and no, No, N, or n (then enter) to select your option. If an error occurs, it
will automatically go away unless it is asking for an input.
